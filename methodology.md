# Methodology

## Research Process

The research process was completed in four stages:

1. Understanding the assignment criteria and defining what qualifies as a “Federer Company”.
2. Using AI tools for initial company discovery and category clustering.
3. Manually verifying shortlisted companies through official websites, LinkedIn, startup databases, and media coverage.
4. Filtering out weak or irrelevant companies before creating the final shortlist.

---

## AI Usage

AI tools were used only as research accelerators and structuring assistants.

AI helped in:

* discovering candidate companies,
* identifying industry clusters,
* organising observations,
* and speeding up initial research.

---

## Hallucination Control

To reduce hallucination risk:

* company headquarters were manually verified,
* unsupported claims were removed,
* multiple sources were cross-checked,
* and generic outsourcing firms were filtered out.

I also rewrote overly polished AI-generated descriptions into more factual and grounded observations.

---

## Key Observations

During the research process, I observed that Bengaluru’s strongest deeptech companies are concentrated around:

* robotics,
* aerospace,
* industrial AI,
* electric mobility,
* and advanced manufacturing.

Many of these companies combine hardware systems with proprietary software and operate in process-intensive environments where systems thinking and operational execution are critical.
